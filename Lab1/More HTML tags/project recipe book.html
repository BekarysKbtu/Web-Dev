Sushi

Ingredient Quantity
Sushi rice 2 cups
Rice vinegar 1/2 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Salt 1 tbsp
Seaweed sheets 5
Fresh fish (e.g., salmon, tuna) 200g
Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado) 1 cup

Details:

  • Preparation time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps:

  1. Cook the sushi rice according to package instructions.
  2. Mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, and fold into the cooked rice.
  3. Place a seaweed sheet on a bamboo mat, spread the rice evenly, and add fillings.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into bite-sized pieces.

Hamburger

Ingredient Quantity
Ground beef 500g
Burger buns 4
Cheddar cheese 4 slices
Tomato 1, sliced
Lettuce 4 leaves
Onion 1, sliced
Ketchup 4 tbsp
Mustard 4 tbsp

Details:

  • Preparation time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps:

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 patties.
  2. Grill the patties for about 5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  3. Toast the burger buns on the grill.
  4. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, ketchup, and mustard.

Dumplings

Ingredient Quantity
Ground pork 500g
Cabbage 1 cup, finely chopped
Ginger 1 tbsp, grated
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
Sesame oil 1 tbsp
Wonton wrappers 30

Details:

  • Preparation time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps:

  1. Mix the ground pork, cabbage, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and sesame oil in a bowl.
  2. Place a small spoonful of the mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the wrappers.
  4. Steam or boil the dumplings until cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
